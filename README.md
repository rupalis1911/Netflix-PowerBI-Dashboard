Key Insights

Netflix content spans 42 distinct genres, indicating a diversified catalog.
TV Thrillers emerge as the top genre among TV Shows.
Movies dominate overall volume, especially in International Movies, Dramas, and Comedies.
TV Shows tend to cluster in specific genres, while Movies cover a broader range.
